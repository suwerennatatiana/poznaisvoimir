Children’s Author — Tatiana Belozub

Stories about nature, harmony, kindness, and the magic of everyday life

About the Author

My name is Tatiana Belozub. I write children’s books that help kids grow up gentle, curious, brave, and connected with the living world around them.

My work is inspired by family, motherhood, real life experiences, and a deep belief: every child carries a natural gift — the ability to love, feel, create, and see beauty in simple things. Through my stories, I want to support this gift, not replace it with empty noise.

In my books, children explore nature, learn to notice patterns, understand why every living being has value, and discover that harmony begins inside our hearts and families.

These stories are gentle, positive, and meaningful — yet simple enough for young readers to understand and enjoy.

Book Series: “Know Your World”

I am developing a long-term series called “Know Your World”. Each book focuses on an essential element of life — Earth, Water, Trees, Animals, Seasons, Energy, Creativity, and more.

The first book is titled:
“Mother Earth — Know Your World”.

Children discover:

  • why nature lives in balance,
  • how crystals, water, trees, and animals interact,
  • why kindness and respect are real strength,
  • how family love makes the world feel safe and bright.

The goal is not simply to “teach facts”, but to awaken awareness, gratitude, responsibility, and joy.

My Creative Vision

I believe children’s literature should heal, inspire, and grow together with the child. Books are more than entertainment — they are gentle guides that shape worldview, emotional resilience, values, and empathy.

Every story I create carries three foundations:

  • Family: the safest place where love begins.
  • Nature: our teacher, friend, and home.
  • Meaning: every page has a purpose.

My dream is to build a large collection of positive books that children will remember — and that parents will want to read together with them.
